Careers at S.L. Raheja Hospital - A Fortis Associate

Join Mumbai’s Leading
Tertiary Care Hospital

S.L. Raheja Hospital - A Fortis Associate, is a distinguished name in the field of tertiary care, offering world-class treatments across various specialties through its Centres of Excellence. Located in the suburbs of Mumbai, our hospital is known not just for exceptional patient care but also for being a rewarding and enriching workplace for its employees.

At S.L. Raheja Hospital, we follow a 5-POINT Value System that forms the core of our culture and drives our operations:

Patient Centricity

Ownership

Integrity

Innovation

Teamwork

With NABH accreditation and numerous awards, we are committed to patient care and employee development.

Recognition Programs

We value and celebrate our employees through:

  • ‘Dil Jeet Liya Aapne’ Card: Spot recognition for going above and beyond.

  • Patient Accolades: Awards for exceptional care recognised by patients.

  • Long Service Awards: Celebrating loyalty and milestones.

  • Star Performers: Quarterly awards for outstanding contributions.

Why Work with Us?

Join a legacy of excellence in healthcare.

Thrive in a culture of growth, integrity, and teamwork.

Benefit from recognition and learning opportunities.

Be a part of S.L. Raheja Hospital - A Fortis Associate, where your efforts are valued, and your career flourishes.

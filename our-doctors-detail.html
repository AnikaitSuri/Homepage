Introduction:

Dr. Haresh Mehta is a distinguished figure in the field of interventional cardiology, serving as the Director of Interventional & Structural Cardiology at S. L. Raheja Hospital - A Fortis Associate, Mumbai. With a career spanning over two decades, Dr. Haresh Mehta has amassed a wealth of experience and expertise, having performed over 10,000 independent interventional cases (angiography, angioplasty etc.), with a majority conducted through the transradial route.

Renowned as a PROCTOR/TRAINER in the field of Transcatheter Aortic Valve Replacement (TAVR), Dr. Haresh Mehta has trained extensively for leading companies such as Medtronic and Meril Valve. He also serves as an Independent Operator for Edwards and Hydra Valves. Notably, Dr. Haresh Mehta achieved the milestone of performing the first double valve in valve replacement transcutaneously in Maharashtra, marking the second such procedure in India. Additionally, he successfully implanted the first Impella (LV SUPPORT DEVICE) in a nonagenarian patient.

Dr. Haresh Mehta's pursuit of excellence has taken him across the globe for specialized training. He honed his skills in coronary and paediatric interventions at University Hospital, Bern, Switzerland, and served as visiting faculty at the same institution. Furthermore, he received training in peripheral, carotid, and renal interventions at the prestigious Lenox Hill Hospital, New York, renowned for its leadership in interventional cardiology. Dr. Haresh Mehta perfected his skill of Rotational Atherectomy at Kings Hospital, London, and Hanusch University Hospital, Vienna, Austria. He also received training in Budapest for TAVI and other Structural Heart Diseases.

Dr. Haresh Mehta is one of the best cardiologist in Mumbai and is actively involved with various professional bodies, including the American College of Cardiology, European Society of Cardiology, Society of Cardiovascular Angiography and Intervention, Cardiology Society of India, Indian College of Cardiology, and the Indian Society of Echocardiography. He has made significant contributions to the field through presentations in journals both in India and abroad. Dr. Haresh Mehta's commitment to advancing the standards of interventional cardiology and structural heart care makes him a leader in his field.
